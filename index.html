Your Guide Through Middle-earth

Contract law is the law of promises. In this course companion, you will experience doctrine as a narrative — from Bilbo's first bargain in the Shire through the Fellowship's complex agreements to the remedies that make Middle-earth whole again.

Seven modules. Twenty-eight chapters. One continuous story.

The Journey

Follow the Hero's Journey from the Shire to Mount Doom. Each module is a stage in the adventure — and a pillar of contract law.
